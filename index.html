You've made it to my page, welcome! I'm a PhD student working at the intersection of causality and neuroimaging. This page will give you an overview of my research, updates, and more fun stuff...

I'm currently pursuing my PhD at King's College London, where I'm advised by Jorge Cardoso and Sebastien Ourselin. My research explores causal discovery and causal representation learning with applications to multi-modal Alzheimer's disease data. My PhD project is part of the Digital Twins for Health CDT at KCL and is kindly co-funded by Siemens Healthineers.

In my spare time, I enjoy life drawing, reading philosophy books and going to the gym (and the occasional reality TV binge).
